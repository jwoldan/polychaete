Polychaete!

Polychaete is inspired by the Centipede arcade game, originally released in 1980. Click here for a short video about Centipede and the woman who helped develop it.

Polychaete was created by Jeffrey Woldan. The source code for Polychaete is available at Github.

Instructions

Shoot the sea creatures to earn points. Avoid them and drop bombs when they enter your area. See how long you can last!

Controls

Left arrow / AMove left
Right arrow / DMove right
Up arrow / WMove up
Down arrow / SMove down
Space barFire laser
ShiftDrop bomb
